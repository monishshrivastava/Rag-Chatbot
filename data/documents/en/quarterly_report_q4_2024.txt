TechCorp Inc. - Q4 2024 Quarterly Financial Report

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
TechCorp Inc. reported strong financial performance for the fourth quarter of 2024, with revenue growth of 18% year-over-year and improved profitability across all business segments.

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS
- Total Revenue: $125.6 million (Q4 2024) vs $106.4 million (Q4 2023)
- Net Income: $28.3 million vs $21.7 million (30% increase)
- Earnings Per Share (EPS): $1.42 vs $1.09 (30% increase)
- Operating Margin: 22.5% vs 20.4% (210 basis points improvement)
- Cash and Cash Equivalents: $89.2 million as of December 31, 2024

BUSINESS SEGMENT PERFORMANCE
Cloud Services Division:
- Revenue: $78.4 million (62% of total revenue)
- Growth: 25% year-over-year
- Key driver: Enterprise customer acquisition increased by 40%

Software Licensing:
- Revenue: $32.1 million (26% of total revenue)
- Growth: 8% year-over-year
- Stable recurring revenue base with 95% customer retention

Professional Services:
- Revenue: $15.1 million (12% of total revenue)
- Growth: 12% year-over-year
- Expanded consulting offerings in AI and machine learning

OUTLOOK FOR 2025
Management expects continued growth momentum with projected revenue of $520-540 million for fiscal year 2025, representing 15-20% growth. The company plans to invest heavily in R&D and expand international operations.
